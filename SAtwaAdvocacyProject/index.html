About My Topic

There are many underpiveleged students and minorities that dont have access too educational resources. This is a problem because these underpriveleged students and minorities are not able to get the education they need to be able to get a job or get a better life. My Educational Outreach Program aims to close the gap between the priveleged and underpriveleged students by offering free educational resources to all students.
